THE FINAL TAKE

Some cameras capture more than just memories.

SYNOPSIS

Three film students—Jake, Bill, and Leo—arrive at their campus at night to shoot a short film for a class project. When their fourth member, Ryan, mysteriously cancels, he leaves behind a digital camera for them to use.

What they discover on the camera is disturbing—photos with distorted faces and unexplainable phenomena. As they begin their shoot, strange occurrences start happening around them.

One by one, they encounter a terrifying entity that transforms them into faceless beings. What started as a simple school project becomes a nightmare that none of them can escape.

THE CURSE

The digital camera holds a dark secret—it doesn't just capture images, it captures souls. Anyone photographed by the camera is marked, their face slowly erased from existence as they become part of the growing collection of faceless entities.

THE CYCLE

Ryan thought he could escape by passing the camera to his friends, but the curse follows a pattern. As the film concludes, we discover that the cycle is endless—the camera always finds its way to new victims.

THE WARNING

"Some cameras capture more than just memories. Some things are better left uncaptured."
